ABSTRACT

  • This project is a web application containing deatails of Orphanage Home.

  • This application is developed mainly for orphan home center to manage the orphan registration and maintainance.

  • The main users of the system are the staff and administrator or manager.

  • only administrators have authority for maintanance such as backup and recovery if there is failure.

  • The details of the orphan and their health conditions are also updated automatically by the managers or administrators.
